FAQ

Questions we hear often

Is chiropractic treatment painful?

No. It is never a "no pain, no gain" situation. Some patients may feel minor discomfort depending on how much inflammation and tension is present. Dr. Melissa takes time to listen and ensure treatments are as comfortable as possible.

What is that popping sound during an adjustment?

A tiny gas bubble gets released from your joint as pressure changes during an adjustment, exactly like cracking your knuckles. Completely normal, and adjustments do not always produce the sound.

Does my medical aid cover chiropractic?

We are not contracted into medical aid, which means we do not claim directly from medical aid schemes. However, our rates are below standard medical aid rates, and once you have paid, we will send you an invoice via email to submit yourself. It is your responsibility to confirm with your scheme whether you are eligible for reimbursement.

How does payment work?

We accept card and cash. Payment must be settled immediately after your session. You will receive an invoice via email afterward, which you can submit to your medical aid yourself for possible reimbursement.

What is your cancellation policy?

We have a strict 24-hour cancellation policy. Any appointment not cancelled within 24 hours of the scheduled time will be billed and charged in full.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies from person to person depending on your condition and how your body responds to treatment. Dr. Melissa will give you an honest assessment after your initial consultation. Some patients feel significant relief after one or two sessions; others benefit from an ongoing maintenance plan.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is ideal. Dr. Melissa will always explain what is needed and make sure you are comfortable throughout.

Where are you and is there parking?

We are on the 1st floor at 27 Derry Street, Vredehoek, above Bella Italia, inside the shared practice known as The Cape Doctors. Street parking is available on Derry Street and surrounding roads. Easy to reach from the City Bowl, Gardens, Tamboerskloof, and De Waal Drive.
